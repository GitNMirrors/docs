Jindiao JD3040¶

Specs¶

  • 30cm x 40cm cutting area
  • 40W CO2 Laser

Motion Card¶

LNC-LS232A HMI: PAD1000-EN

Mecanics¶

  • X axis:

    Direct drive NEMA 23 2/4 Phase 1,8° Stepper “57BYGH-209B-16” Leadshine M542-05 Driver

  • Y axis:

    Reduction belt NEMA 23 2/4 Phase 1,8° Stepper

  • Z axis:

    Circular belt NEMA 23 2/4 Phase 1,8° Stepper “57BHH56-300B-21”

  • 25mm Lens 1” focal distance
